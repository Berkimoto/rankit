Common Questions

  • How do I cancel my subscription? — Open the App Store → your Apple ID → Subscriptions → RankIt → Cancel.
  • I lost my progress after reinstalling. — Sign in with the same Google or Apple account you used before.
  • My score didn't update. — Check your internet connection and reopen the app.
  • How do I restore purchases? — Go to Settings → Premium → Restore Purchases.
PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Information We Collect

  • Account information (name, email, profile photo) when you sign in with Google, Apple, or Facebook.
  • Gameplay data (scores, rankings, game history).
  • Your selected country flag for leaderboard display.
  • Device information for analytics and crash reporting.
  • Push notification tokens to send you reminders and game invites.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the RankIt service.
  • To display your username, country flag, and scores on global, country, and friends leaderboards. This information is visible to other players.
  • To enable multiplayer and friends features — if you add friends, your scores and rankings will be visible to them.
  • To send push notifications (which you can disable in device settings).
  • To show relevant advertisements (free users only).

Leaderboard & Data Visibility

By using RankIt, you agree that your username, country flag, and game scores may appear on:

  • Global Leaderboard — visible to all RankIt players worldwide.
  • Country Leaderboard — visible to players from the same country.
  • Friends Leaderboard — visible to players you have connected with as friends.

You are informed of this during the onboarding process and must agree before your data is uploaded. You can delete your account at any time to remove your data from leaderboards.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, analytics.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management.
  • Google AdMob — advertisements for free users.

Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from within the app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
